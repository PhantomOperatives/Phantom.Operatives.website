Rules

  1. In situations with hostages, and try to do it always use "SEMI"
  2. Go as quickly as safety as you can
  3. If you have a target at your front but your teammate is also at your front, don't aim him, find another movement.
  4. When entring, if its not "Y" position, we will go like a cross, if you start from the side right, you will enter and go to the left, the same thing the other way around.
  5. Every room must be cleaned by two or more people (if possible)
  6. The shooting time you have against the enemy is 0.01

Basic Breaching System

Leader's words

  • ? = Are you guys ready?
  • ! = Start breaching
  • X = Front to Front Line
  • Y = Single line behind Leader

Teammates's words

  • . = ready
  • , = not ready

Everyone's words

  • C = This room/small area is clear
  • AC = All the area is clear
  • R = Reloading (Just in difficult cases)
  • MF = Mission Finish
  • NH = Need help









Advanced Breaching System

Positions/Mechanics

  • X = Two lines between the door
  • Y = Single file line behind the leader
  • U = Like "X" but 2 more people next to the leaders
  • POI = Check for enemies with a safety method
  • FOI = Say "yes" with the head means ready
