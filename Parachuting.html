Parachuting


Explanation

The yellow is the leader, when parachuting the leader will say if you can jump or
not. First of all, when the light is green, everybody gets up and makes a single line,
SFL, behind the leader. Then the leader will inspect the terrain and if it's good to
jump he will start, the leader moves to a side and the others start jumping, one by
one but quickly, G to open a parachute, finally when everybody has jumped the
leader jumps.



  • HALO High Altitude Low Opening (50/200f)
  • HAHO High Altitude High Opening (1000/3000f)
