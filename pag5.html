Rules



  1. All member of this organisation are your brother in arms they should not be left behind in combat or training they are a reason you are alive from your previous mission.
  2. Be honest and truthful in all situations, even when it is most difficult for your fellow brothers in arm.
  3. Remember not all decisions are easy some may be obvious to do some are not, and it is always bad and worse try to make the best choice.
  4. Working together with others to achieve a common goal, and recognizing the value of everyone's contributions are the piece components of teamwork.
  5. Do not show weakness in any situation or you will be showing your ennemy where to strike to make you collapse.
  6. Do not step back against any mission or challenge, it is only one more obstacle in your path.
