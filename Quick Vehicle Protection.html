Quick Vehicle Protection



Ground Vehicle Protection

  • Formation 1

    • Formation 1 is based on forming a semicircle next to the ground vehicle, simply making this semicircle,
    everyone must look at all sides, this position requires +3/inf people. They must leave 1 at a time,
    the first goes in front of the red dots, the place where they have the most visibility, then the other 2 go,
    one to the left and the other to the right, and the rest complete the circle. They always have to cover all sides.









  • Formation 2

    • Formation 2 is to protect the vehicle, you can also do this with important objects or transports,
    it consists of making a rectangle / square and someone in the turret (optional). They must go out one by one,
    the leader will say who goes out first. Then, each one goes to his position, the first to the front, the last to the back,
    and when leaving, always covering all possible sides.










Ground Vehicle Protection on Movement

  • You Defend Vehicle [YDV]

    • It is based on when you are moving and you have to protect the vehicle, basically it consists of you are defending
    the vehicle, imagine as if the president was inside, you have to surround the entire vehicle and secure it, it can also
    be done with: things / objects / people/vehicles...









  • Vehicle Defends You [VDY]

    • It is based on when you are moving but the vehicle protects you, basically the vehicle protects you from any shot,
    think as if it were your shield, then you go out, do the GPV first, and once prepared, you start with the VDY
    which consists In making two lines behind him, the first two will always look straight ahead, and the others
    have to check the sides and behind (they can also check in front).
